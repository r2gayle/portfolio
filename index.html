About Me

I'm a front-end developer and avid music lover based in Toronto, Canada.

Before my journey into web development I was in the finance industry. The majority of my roles were client serving and it allowed me the opportunity to help them in difficult situations through a combination of logical analysis and creative problem solving. As a developer I've brought that same mindset and enjoy creating accessible and intuitive web applications to give users the best experience. In my free time, I’m always on the hunt for new restaurants or looking for new artist to listen to!
